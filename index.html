About Us

Our key focus is improving labour contract supply for the Oil & Gas industry, commercial and residential maintenance and repair operations in the UAE while providing high-quality services at value-for-money prices. We organise a sector that is not very well represented in the UAE and ensure professional workmanship on all projects, large or small.

Our customer-focused policy is based on long-term client retention and complete satisfaction. We operate across all kinds of residential and commercial maintenance needs — owners, tenants and SMEs — ensuring full satisfaction and building our reputation as a top-rated service provider in UAE maintenance.

Our Mission

To provide knowledgeable, convenient and reasonably priced technicians to the United Arab Emirates, build lifelong relationships with customers, and provide the best services on time and at the customer's convenience.

Our Vision & Values

We get the job done on time, within budget, while providing quality service. We commit to safety, quality personnel, punctuality and customer satisfaction. Our technicians are professional, well-behaved and take pride in their work.

Our Services

End-to-end services to take ideas from concept to reality.

Contract Labor - Oil & Gas

Highly skilled manpower tailored to the Oil & Gas sector: engineers, technicians and field experts with compliance to UAE labor and HSE standards for upstream, midstream and downstream operations.

Contract Labor - Prefab Housing

Skilled manpower for prefab housing projects: assembly, finishing and structural work with adherence to safety and fast mobilisation to meet project timelines.

AC Installation & Maintenance

Installation and servicing for all types of AC systems — single split to VRF/VRV and chiller systems. We provide maintenance to keep systems efficient and extend lifespan.

Plumbing & Sanitary

Professional plumbing installations, maintenance and repairs. Our team handles fixtures, drain issues, water heaters and full sanitary systems with a focus on hygiene and reliability.

Plaster Work

From small repairs to full re-plastering: re-skims, dry lining, rendering and damp proofing with a 100% satisfaction guarantee and tidy work sites.

Carpentry & Flooring

Experienced carpenters for joinery, fitted furniture, flooring and finish carpentry across residential and industrial projects.

Painting Contracting

Industrial and commercial painting, protective coatings and long-lasting finishes for residential and large-scale projects.
